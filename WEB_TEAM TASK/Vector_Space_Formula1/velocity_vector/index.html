About Velocity Vector

Our team is composed of ambitious and talented engineering students who are dedicated to designing and building a high-performance formula-style race car. We compete in the Formula Student competition, where we go up against other universities from all over the world. Here at Velocity Racing, we are passionate about innovation and pushing the boundaries of what's possible. We strive to create a car that is not only fast and agile but also safe and reliable. Our team members work tirelessly to design, fabricate, and test every component of our race car, from the suspension and chassis to the aerodynamics and electronics. We are proud to represent our university on a global stage and showcase our engineering skills to the world. Our team has achieved great success in the past, and we are determined to continue pushing ourselves to new heights. Please explore our website to learn more about our team, our car, and our journey towards excellence. Feel free to contact us if you have any questions or are interested in becoming a part of our team. Join us on this exciting journey, and let's race towards the future together! Thank you for visiting Velocity Racing!.

Being a part of the Formula Student legacy for over a decade

Our F1 legacy has continued for over a decade now and we wish you could be a part of this as well, In India, the Formula Student competition is known as ‘Formula Bharat’ and is held each year in the Kari Motor Speedway racetrack in Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu. More than 50 Indian teams participate each year in the competition to test the mettle of their engineering creation and rack up ideas in the static events. At the end of the day, the teams leave with a lot of new things learned and experience gained, to be back at taking a shot at the podium next year and maybe, get some payback for last time!
